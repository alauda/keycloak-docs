## Release Policy

Alauda Build of Keycloak follows a **rolling support model**:

- The lifecycles of different major versions are independent of each other; multiple major versions may be in maintenance simultaneously.
- Each minor version remains supported **until the next minor version is officially released plus 1 month**. Maintenance is provided through patch releases applied to each supported minor version.

## Maintenance Policy

Alauda provides maintenance support for Alauda Build of Keycloak based on its rolling support model.

During the support period of each version, Alauda provides the following services:

- **Security Updates**: Regular patches and updates aligned with Alauda security standards and upstream Keycloak security advisories.
- **Bug Fixes**: Timely fixes for critical and major issues, aligned with upstream Keycloak community fixes.
- **Upgrade Assistance**: Guidance and support for smooth upgrades between Alauda Build of Keycloak versions.
- **Regular Maintenance**: Periodic updates to ensure the Operator remains stable and compatible with the Kubernetes ecosystem.
